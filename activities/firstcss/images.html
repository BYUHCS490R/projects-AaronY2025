Images

Questions & Answers

  1. Jpeg is good for more clearer images to compress while still making the picture look good, although it may lose quality in the process.
  2. GIF is good for things like icons or logos, as well as short animations. Making it good for short moving images.
  3. PNG is good for compressing photos without losing quality. They also support transparency so it is good for images w/o a background.
  4. Resizing photos is so that the image you are making has the correct pixel dimensions to match the exact size the image should be displaying.
  5. You shouldn't use CSS because it would slow down the entire page. Editing the photo first reduces the file size and improves the performance on your devices.
